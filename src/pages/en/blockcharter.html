What is BlockCharter?

BlockCharter offers you the flexibility to book flight hours in packages and use them according to your needs. Ideal for pilots who want to fly regularly without worrying about individual bookings.

With our BlockCharter packages, you benefit from:

  • Lower hourly rates through package booking
  • Flexible use of your booked hours
  • Priority in aircraft booking
  • Validity over an extended period
  • Perfect flying conditions in Mallorca
