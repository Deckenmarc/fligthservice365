3. Prices and Payment Terms

3.1 Prices

Unless otherwise stated, all prices quoted are final prices including statutory value-added tax. Additional costs such as landing fees, parking fees, or special insurance will be shown and charged separately.

3.2 Payment Terms

Payment is made according to agreement:

  • Aircraft Charter: 50% deposit upon booking confirmation, balance 7 days before flight date
  • BlockCharter: Full payment upon contract conclusion
  • Flight Training (PPL-365): Installment payment possible by arrangement
  • Hour Building: Payment after each flight hour or monthly billing
  • Trial Flights: Full payment before flight departure

3.3 Payment Default

In case of payment default, the Provider is entitled to charge default interest at a rate of 5 percentage points above the base rate. The assertion of higher default damages remains reserved.

4. Booking Conditions

4.1 Booking Requirements

The following requirements apply for booking our services:

  • Aircraft Charter: Minimum age 18 years, valid ID
  • Flight Training: Minimum age 17 years, Class 2 medical certificate
  • Hour Building: Valid pilot license (minimum PPL-A)
  • Trial Flights: No special requirements, minimum age 12 years (with accompaniment)

4.2 Booking Changes

Changes to a confirmed booking are possible subject to availability and must be communicated in writing at least 48 hours before the scheduled flight date. A processing fee of 50 EUR may be charged for booking changes.

5. Cancellation Policy

5.1 Cancellation by Customer

The customer may cancel the booking in writing at any time. The following cancellation fees apply:

  • Up to 30 days before flight date: 10% of total price
  • 29 to 14 days before flight date: 50% of total price
  • 13 to 7 days before flight date: 75% of total price
  • Less than 7 days before flight date or no-show: 100% of total price

Separate cancellation conditions apply for BlockCharter packages and flight training programs, which are specified in the respective contract.

5.2 Cancellation by Provider

The Provider reserves the right to cancel a booking for good cause, particularly in case of:

In such cases, the customer will be informed immediately and will receive the option of free rebooking or full refund of payments already made. Further claims for damages are excluded unless there is intentional or grossly negligent breach of duty.
